<objective>
Eliminate the warmup GET from the cart-add hot path and persist a sessid_ts timestamp at login time so downstream staleness detection can work.

Purpose: The warmup GET to vkusvill.ru takes 2-5s through proxy, making the first cart add exceed the 5s budget. By persisting session metadata at login and refusing to do warmup GETs during cart add, we guarantee the cart POST fires within milliseconds of the request arriving.

Output: Modified vkusvill_api.py that never blocks on warmup GET + main.py that saves sessid_ts at login.
</objective>
