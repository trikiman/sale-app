## Why v1.19

Closed the production drift exposed after v1.18 shipped: 8 days of degraded behavior visible only in EC2 logs (pool 25 → 13, 162 consecutive scraper-cycle failures, 30/30 detail-proxy timeouts) while Vercel `/api/products` returned HTTP 200 with cached data. PR #25 (Devin, 2026-04-29) attempted a 5 s pre-flight probe hotfix, was reverted 8 minutes later by PR #26 because empirical healthy-node latency through the bridge is 7-9 s.

The milestone was explicitly **robust-over-fast** (user directive 2026-05-03): no fast hotfixes — every phase shipped with a scripted EC2 smoke test, a `VERIFICATION.md`, and a rehearsed rollback path. Backend reliability only; user-visible degraded mode (Category G) deferred to v1.20.

**Requirements:** 18 (REL-01..12 reliability, OBS-01..03 observability, OPS-06..08 operations)
**Deferrals to v1.20:** REL-FUT-01..08, OBS-FUT-01..03, OPS-FUT-01..02, UI-FUT-01..04

Full research: `.planning/research/v1.19-SUMMARY.md`.

### Phase 60: Observatory probeURL + Graduated Circuit Breaker (shipped 2026-05-04)

**Goal:** Fix the silent killer — xray observatory probed `google.com/generate_204` while our traffic went to VkusVill, so blocked-by-VkusVill nodes stayed "fast" in `leastPing`. Replaced the cycle-counter breaker with a 3-state machine (closed/open/half_open) + exponential backoff capped at 30 min, persisted across scheduler restarts.

**Requirements satisfied:** REL-06, REL-07, REL-08, REL-09, REL-10 (+ OPS-06/07/08)

**Shipped:**
- `vless/config_gen.py::build_xray_config` emits `observatory.probeURL = "https://vkusvill.ru/favicon.ico"`, `probeInterval = 60s`; regression test asserts hostname matches `*.vkusvill.ru`
- `scheduler_service.py::BreakerState` 3-state machine; cooldown `min(120 × 2^level, 1800)`; counter resets on **any** scraper success
- `data/scheduler_state.json` atomic persistence + corrupt-file fallback
- 7 Phase-60 smoke checks added to `verify_v1.19.sh`
